About Me

Hello! I'm Jason, a passionate Computer Science graduate with a strong foundation in software development and a keen interest in sustainable technology solutions. My academic journey has equipped me with a diverse skill set, from developing web applications to exploring database management.

During my time as a student, I had the opportunity to work on several innovative projects.
